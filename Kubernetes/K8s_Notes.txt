Kubernetes (k8s)
- Enterprise Features: Built-in support for complex networking, storage, and cluster configurations.
- Flexibility: High degree of customization and extensibility.
- Learning Curve: Steeper, more components to manage.
- Ecosystem: Larger community, more third-party integrations.

K3s
- Limited Features: Lacks some advantanced features of Kubernetes, but enough for small-to-medium deployments. 
- Speed: Faster start-up and operation times.
- Lightweight: Minimal resource footprint, good for edge or IoT devices.
